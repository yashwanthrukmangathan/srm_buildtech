Why we Manufacture our own raw material?

Conventionally other RMC buy their BlueMetals and M-Sand from other Suppliers.So, that Maintaining the raw material for concrete is so difficult Especially in the followig cases -
  • White stone mixing with BlueMetals issuses
  • over size of 12mm and 20mm
  • flackiness and elongation problem in BlueMetals
  • silt content in M-sand, Grading of M-sand
  • dusting problem in BlueMetals.
This problems are directly affect the Quality of concrete and Durability of concrete.To overcome this challenge We directly Manufacturing Raw Materials as per IS Standard.By this We Manufacture Quality and Durable concrete.If not maintaining this parameters there are several problems facing in post-concreting.Mainly, crack which leads to leakage in roofslab.

How we Produce Crackless Concrete ?

As we mentioned in above issuse we directly Manufacture our own raw material from our own Crushers.By This all the parameters which leads cracks is minimized and control .so, that we can easily produce crackless concrete.

We strongly believe that the raw material plays the major role in a Quality of a concrete.So, We have the own Crusher plant.So, We can maintain our Quality of the concrete regular than others.
